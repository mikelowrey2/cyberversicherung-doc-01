Getting Started with Our Platform

October 28, 2025
8 min read

Ransomware attacks threaten medical practices through encrypted files and extortion demands. Cybercriminals target practices for sensitive patient data, limited IT resources, and willingness to pay ransoms. Attacks cause devastating financial losses, operational disruptions, and reputation damage.

Prevention requires regular software updates, employee training, multi-factor authentication, reliable backups, and network segmentation. However, specialized cyber insurance for medical practices provides essential protection, covering ransomware losses, business interruption, data recovery, forensic investigations, and legal support.

Combining robust technical security measures with comprehensive cyber insurance creates effective defense against evolving threats, safeguarding both practice operations and patient data.
